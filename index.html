Technology has evoloved so much over time. Let's backtrack and see where the current technology came from.

The first telephone was the stepping stone to many other forms of technology. The first telephone was invented by Alexander Graham Bell. The telephone was based off of the idea of the telegraph, however the telegraph could only receive and send one message at a time. The telephone was a device that allowed speech to be carried over electric currents. Bell and his partner created a transmitter and a membrane with the ability to carry speech over the wires. On June 2, 1875 the first telephone call was made, and it was a complete accident. This was a ground breaking milestone because now people could communicate at any time they wanted to from anywhere. This led to a telephone network all over the country. Telephone lines were being built and the demand for electronic communication quickly became a staple in everyday lives. The discovery of the telephone allowed people to invent other innovative forms of technology, and showed that their idea could soon become a reality.
